  • Multiple miscommunication instances has led to conflicts between divisions.
  • We were tasked to recommend ideas to optimize internal processes.

3. Data Transparency

Lack of project pipeline awareness
  1. Lack of knowledge about other divisionsâ€™ projects.
  2. Lack of cross division beneficial sharing of ideas.
  3. Ad hoc task assignment without reasoning.
  4. Unawareness of teammate skills.
Clients are managed individually
  1. Multiple disconnected client lists and histories in different CRMs.
  2. Limited sharing of client information.
  3. Lost opportunities to collaborate and share knowledge.
Implement company-wide project and client management systems.
  1. Projects tracked and visible to all employees.
  2. Employee skill set profiles and roles.
  3. Task assignment.
  4. History of past and present projects and contacts.

4. Physical Space

Physical space layout enhanced already siloed divisions
  1. 2 floors discourage employee interaction, some do not see each other for days.
  2. No space for workshops, brainstorming and general meetings.
  3. Meeting spaces are either too small (3-4 people) or too big (20+ people).
Consolidate all divisions into same floor
  1. Enough floor area to support all divisions.
  2. Second floor dedicated to administration, meetings and collaboration spaces.

5. Company & Personal Goals

Siloed staff meetings and unclear company goals
  1. Management updates happen per division basis.
  2. Presentations focused on personal goals, nor project or company goals.
  3. Limited time and lack of feedback.
  4. Time wasted on the wrong topics.
Reserve company topics to staff meetings and personal goals to one-on-ones
  1. Encourage discourse about company issues and goals.
  2. Reserve meetings for project in-depth discussions.
  3. Use one-on-ones for personal goal assessments.
