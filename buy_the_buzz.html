1. Context & Problem

  • Young working professionals aspire to dress in style but do not have time to follow trends.
  • Based on research outlined by a third party agency, the client asked to design a conceptual app with the goal of selling fashion through stylist recommendations.

2. Brainstorming

Collecting User Data
  • An app that recommends fashion based on taste suggests some sort of user data needs to be collected for personalization.
  • Other than usual body measurement and fashion choices, interaction data such as completion rate, number of steps, length of consultations, spending patterns, errors and mistakes also suggests places to explore further.
Ecommerce Logistics
  • Shopping is one of the revenue sources for the app. However, as a startup, the company does not have the resources to manage an entire ecommerce business.
  • One alternative is relying on affiliate links and earning commission.
  • This also means the choice of clothing would be limited to the affiliate network offerings.
Stylist Consultation
  • Paying to access stylists recommendations is a second source of income for the app.
  • For the stylists, there needs to be a balance between earning and time dedicated to the platform. Growing the user-base might need to come first.
  • A backend tool for stylists is also needed to manage conversations.
  • Consultations won’t start from zero, as commonly practiced in the industry, users would likely start by looking for something to pair with existing wardrobe pieces.
Supporting Content
  • Other content needs to be provided and updated daily for users to come back every day.
  • Editorial staff and curation services will be needed to support the content.
  • To lessen the workload, certain users would be allowed to publish pieces for self-promotion. Emerging designers, stylists and other related professionals come to mind.

3. Proposal

Application Map
  • Four main areas to support personal-attention service vs general-browsing activities.
  • A separate webapp is proposed for stylists to manage consultations, browse affiliate products and access client preference data.
  • Separate CMS for admins to manage app content, analytics, finances and user moderation is planned for future discussion.

Onboarding & Registration
  • Optional onboarding screens to briefly expose users to main features of the app.
  • Lengthy questionnaire to document user data to personalize content.
  • All onboarding and registration steps need to be optional to allow unobstructed access to the content.


Consultation
  • Uses a chat interface where stylists follow a list of parameters when interacting with users to maintain a consistent experience.
  • Consultations cost tokens called “sessions”, which are purchased in-app either singularly or in packs.
  • To bridge the virtual and physical world, in-person consultations are also available.

Supportive Content
  • Ten product recommendations are posted per day, which is the only in-house editorial provided.
  • Extended articles and promotion pieces are created by stylists and fashion professionals, under performance guidelines of the company.

4. Future Considerations

If the goal is saving professional women’s time by offering curated matching pieces, then a personalized curation service seems to be more fitting at first. Editorial and supporting content can be pushed to a later phase as the brand explores its personality.

Enough users need to be present to support stylists. User acquisition can be done through traditional methods such as free tokens, referrals or discounts. Additionally, migrating existing clients to the platform is also feasible.

Certain practices in the stylist’s profession can be merged into the app, such as allowing users to upload their wardrobe for stylists to use as a starting point. Digital and physical worlds can be merged by, for example, mailing large paper backdrops to use when taking wardrobe photos. Such implementations not only makes good marketing, but also creates user convenience and cleans up raster data for post-processing.
